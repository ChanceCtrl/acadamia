# Manual Lathe
Tool can move in
- X-axis
- Y
- Z
All movements are done by hand

Spindle & chuck: Rod that makes it spin & clamp to the work piece
Tail-stock: Another clamp (for long pieces)
Tool Post: Its where your tool goes
