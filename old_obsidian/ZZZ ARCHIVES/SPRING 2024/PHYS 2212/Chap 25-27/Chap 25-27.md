### Series
In series, current is common => $I_{1} = I_{2} = I$
thus $V = V_{1} + V_{2} = IR_{1} + IR_{2} = I (R_{1} + R_{2})$
or simply $V = I \Sigma R_{i}$

### Parallel
In parallel, voltage is common => $I = I_{1} + I_{2} = \frac{V}{R_{1}} + \frac{V}{R_{2}}$
or simply $\frac{1}{R} = \Sigma\frac{1}{R_{i}}$
