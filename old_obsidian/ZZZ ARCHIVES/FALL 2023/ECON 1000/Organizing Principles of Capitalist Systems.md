#econ1000 

Ep = Price
Eq = Quantity

# Isolated Positive Demand Shift
As a result of demand shifting to the right
1. Ep increases
2. Eq increases

# Isolated Negative Demand Shift
As a result of demand shifting to the left
1. Ep decreases
2. Eq decreases

# As Supply Increases
- At any price the new Qs is greater than the previous Qs (i.e shift to the right).

# As Supply Decreases
- At any price the new Qs is less than the previous Qs. (i.e shift to the left)

# Factors that Change/Shift the Supply Curve
**ROTTEN**
- **R**esource cost- the cost of any [[Factors of Production]]
- **O**ther goods - Price of Complement or Substitute
- **T**echnology - (improvements)
- **T**axes & Subsidies (encouragements/discouragements)
- **E**xpectations (of future prices)
- **N**umber of Sellers (market size)

# Isolated Positive Supply Shift
As a result of supply shifting to the right
- Ep decreases
- Eq increases

# Isolated Negative Supply Shift
As a result of supply shifting to the left
- Ep increases
- Eq decreases

# The Role of Entrepreneurs
