### Centrally-Planned Economy
- Government ownership of all resources ([[Factors of Production]])
### Distribution of income & output
- Decisions handled by central planning board

## Argument supporting Communism
Stateless, classless economic system where all the [[Factors of Production]] are owned and controlled by the people.

# Key player is my boi [[Karl Marx]]

# Flaws
- Resources are often used inefficiently or wasted.
- Central planners make all the decisions
- Limited variety of products
- Less freedom of economic choice
- Reduced incentives to develop better products
