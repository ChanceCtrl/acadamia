## Store of value
- Asset that **holds** its value over time. (e.g, money/valuables stored in a vault)

## Unit of measure
- Measures or compares economic activity (e.g, mile measures distance)

# Demand
Relationship between "price (P)" on the y axis and "quantity demanded (Q)" on the x axis

# [[Buyer's Reservation Price]]
## Maximum dollar amount a buyer is willing to pay (WTP) in order to acquire an item

## CHANGE IN PRICE CAUSES MOVEMENT ALONG THE DEMAND CURVE & DOES _NOT_ SHIFT/CHANGE THE DEMAND CURVE

# [[Seller's Reservation Price]]
## Minimum dollar amount a seller is willing to sell (WTS) in order to part with an item.
- Note: At any point on the supply curve this price indicates the lowest amount consumers are willing to buy

# Supply
Relationship between price (P) & quantity supplied (QS)
