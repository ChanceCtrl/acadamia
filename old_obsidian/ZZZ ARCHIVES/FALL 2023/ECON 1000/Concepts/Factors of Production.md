# FOP 
The services and resources that [[Households]] produce and [[Firms]] consume.

## At any point in time, a society is endowed with a limited amount of productive resources.
- Land
	- Natural resources *(land, water oil & minerals.)*
- Labor
	- Individual's work, time & effort.
- Capital
	- Physical 
		- Tools, machinery, equipment & tech
	- Human
		- Entrepreneurial ability, knowledge, skill, & ideas.
- Entrepreneurs
	- See Human captical


## Goods & Services
- The 'output' (tangible/intagible) of the production process.

## Firms
Decision makers whose primary economic objective is to produce Goods & Services for consumption

## Households
Supply FOPs

# Resource
## Payments
Land -> Rent
Labor -> Wages
Physical Capital -> Interest
Entrepreneurship -> Profit

# Maximizing Economic Surplus
Value is created for society if a household's benefit from demand is greater than the burden of supplying the factors of production.
